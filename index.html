Innovation

1 / 5
A UK-based company called BioCarbon are using drones to spray tree seeds across damaged forests. They claim to plant 1 billion trees per year!
2 / 5
Engineer Eric Loth invented palm-tree like turbines. They are twice as large as regular ones. With their 650 foot blades, they would function like palm tree fronds by adapting to the speed and direction of the wind. A wind farm the size of Greenland using these turbines could possibly generate enough energy to power all of humanity.
3 / 5
The Enviornmental Defense Fund is planning to build/launch a satellie that can detect industrial methane leaks. If successful, it will reduce emissions by 50%.
4 / 5
A team of researchers in Japan developed an enzyme called PETase. It can break down plastic in a matter of days!
5 / 5
Recently developed solar panels in China are able to harvest energy from raindrops! This enables power to flow even when clouds cover the sun, or when the sun has set.
